Tacos Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of sliced green onions

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and water to the skillet, stirring to combine.
  3. Simmer for 5-10 minutes, or until the mixture has thickened.
  4. While the beef is cooking, warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave.
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing a spoonful of the beef mixture in the center of each tortilla.
  6. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, cilantro, and green onions.
  7. Fold the tortillas in half and serve immediately.
