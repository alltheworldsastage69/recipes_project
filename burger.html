Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, or any other desired toppings

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix until well combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
  4. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness is reached.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the bottom buns and topping with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, or any other desired toppings.
  8. Place the top buns on the burgers and serve immediately.
