Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup of sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup of sliced olives
  • 1/4 cup of sliced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of sliced basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses over the sauce.
  6. Top with pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, and tomatoes.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with basil leaves.
  9. Slice and serve hot.
